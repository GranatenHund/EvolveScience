Celestial Bodies

Celestial bodies or heavenly bodies are objects in space such as the sun, moon, planets, and stars. They form a part of the vast universe we live in and are usually very far from us.

What is EvolvedScience?

EvolveScience is a website that contains information regarding cosmic objects such as active galaxies, black holes, stars, planets, asteroids, and their descriptions and examples within.

Common Example Cosmic Bodies:

Galaxies

A system of millions or billions of stars, together with gas and dust, held together by gravitational attraction.

Black Holes

A region of space having a gravitational field so intense that no matter or radiation can escape.

Stars

Planets

Asteriods
